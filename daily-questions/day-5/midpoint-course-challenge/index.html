Midpoint Course Challenge!

Register your team, then enter your code to unlock Round 1. Solve to advance — each round reveals the next.

How the relay works

  • You're in a team of up to six. Work together! Set up a google doc to share all of the passcodes you'll need to keep track of to move thrugh this together
  • The relay runs in rounds. Each round has a set of ciphers — solve enough of them to unlock the next round.
  • Every cipher you solve reveals a password. Enter your passwords on that round's google form to officially submit your answers. You can't edit a round once it's submitted, so submit when you're done.
  • More solves means more points, and the first teams to finish earn a time bonus.
  • Only one person has to submit the google form every round. They must retrieve the password from the form, and share it to others / write it down to proceed!
  1. 1

    Register your team

    Add everyone in your group and submit. Your confirmation screen shows a team code — you'll need it in step 2.

  2. 2

    Enter your code to begin

    Type the team code from your confirmation screen to unlock the first round. No button appears until the code is right.
