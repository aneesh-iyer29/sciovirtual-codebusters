Midpoint Course Challenge!

The relay is over — every round is now open. Jump into any round below and solve at your own pace.

What this was

  • The midpoint course challenge was a live relay race: teams of up to six raced through five rounds of ciphers.
  • Each round has a set of ciphers — during the event, solving enough of them unlocked the next round.
  • The competition is over, so the codes and forms are gone. The ciphers themselves are all still here to practice on — each one checks your answer live.
